<p>1.</p><br>
<p>When Do Recreation When Idle appeared on the Workshop, I couldn't help but shout with excitement.</p><br>
<p>It was exactly the mod I'd been looking for.</p><br>
<br>
<p>2.</p><br>
<p>However, the way the mod worked was slightly different from what I had in mind.</p><br>
<p>I got some ideas from the original mod's mechanics and created this one with Xercaine's permission.</p><br>
<br>
<p>3.</p>
<p>Here's what I discovered: There are two types of recreation scenarios.</p><br>
<p>Colonists will engage in recreation during work if their Joy drops too low.</p><br>
<p>They'll also do recreation when idle if their Joy is below 90%.</p><br>
<br>
<p>4.<p>
<p>So, if we prevent Joy from reaching 90%, colonists will keep doing recreation while idle.</p><br>
<p>Since the maximum mood buff occurs at 85% Joy, I reduced the Joy gain to 1/1000 when Joy is above 87.5%.</p><br>
<p>(which is the midpoint between 85% and 90%)</p><br>
<br>
<p>5.</p>
<p>If colonists ever reach 100% Joy, they might wander aimlessly for a while.</p><br>
<p>To prevent this, I increased the Joy decay rate by 10x when Joy is above 90%.</p><br>
<p>This means any wandering will be very brief, and we can watch our colonists engage in endless recreation.</p><br>
<br>
<p>6.</p><br>
<p>Colonists will take recreation breaks during work if their Joy drops too low.</p><br>
<p>Since the Joy peak is now 12.5% lower, I multiplied the Joy decay rate by 0.875.</p><br>
<p>This ensures colonists won't get bored more quickly than before.</p><br>
<br>
<p>7.</p><br>
<p>Since this mod only affects Joy gain and decay rates,</p><br>
<p>it's compatible with any recreation mod out there.</p><br>
<br>
<p>8.</p><br>
<p>All of this was made possible thanks to Xercaine's permission to modify their work. Thank you!</p><br>
