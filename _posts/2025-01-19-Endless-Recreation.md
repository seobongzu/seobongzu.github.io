<p><b>1.</b></p>
<p>When <a href="https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3368214339" target="_blank">Do Recreation When Idle</a> appeared on the Workshop, I couldn't help but shout with excitement.</p>
<p>It was exactly the mod I'd been looking for.</p>
<br>
<p><b>2.</b></p>
<p>However, the way the mod worked was slightly different from what I had in mind.</p>
<p>I got some ideas from the original mod's mechanics and created this one with <a href="https://steamcommunity.com/profiles/76561198062265145/myworkshopfiles/?appid=294100" traget="_blank">Xercaine</a>'s permission.</p>
<br>
<p><b>3.</b></p>
<p>Here's what I discovered: There are two types of recreation scenarios.</p>
<p>Colonists will engage in recreation during work if their Joy drops too low.</p>
<p>They'll also do recreation when idle if their Joy is below 90%.</p>
<br>
<p><b>4.</b><p>
<p>So, if we prevent Joy from reaching 90%, colonists will keep doing recreation while idle.</p>
<p>Since the maximum mood buff occurs at 85% Joy, I reduced the Joy gain to 1/1000 when Joy is above 87.5%.</p>
<p>(which is the midpoint between 85% and 90%)</p>
<br>
<p><b>5.</b></p>
<p>If colonists ever reach 100% Joy, they might wander aimlessly for a while.</p>
<p>To prevent this, I increased the Joy decay rate by 10x when Joy is above 90%.</p>
<p>This means any wandering will be very brief, and we can watch our colonists engage in endless recreation.</p>
<br>
<p><b>6.</b></p>
<p>Colonists will take recreation breaks during work if their Joy drops too low.</p>
<p>Since the Joy peak is now 12.5% lower, I multiplied the Joy decay rate by 0.875.</p>
<p>This ensures colonists won't get bored more quickly than before.</p>
<br>
<p><b>7.</b></p>
<p>Since this mod only affects Joy gain and decay rates,</p>
<p>it's compatible with any recreation mod out there.</p>
