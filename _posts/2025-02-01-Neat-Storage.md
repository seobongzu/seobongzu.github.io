<p><b>1.</b></p>
<p>I don’t dislike the vanilla Shelf, but having the same one in every room felt a bit dull.</p>
<br>
<p><b>2.</b></p>
<p>I searched for a suitable Storage mod to replace it, but I couldn’t find one that I liked.</p>
<p>There were many excellent mods, but I prefer not to have items hidden from view, so I decided to create my own.</p>
<br>
<p><b>3.</b></p>
<p>Since the items are always visible, I designed the furniture to be as simple as possible.</p>
<p>If you look closely, you’ll notice that all of its elements are rectangular.</p>
<p>The furniture itself might seem plain, but this simplicity helps the items stand out.</p>
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/OUCdD1b.png">
<br>
<p><b>4.</b></p>
<p>With 100 RimWorld players, each experiencing the game differently, balancing was a challenge.</p>
<p>In the end, I prioritized good visuals and provided various options.</p>
<br>
<p><b>5.</b></p>
<p>Now, I’m refining the visual details while playing the game myself.</p>
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/ZKpIQx7.png">
